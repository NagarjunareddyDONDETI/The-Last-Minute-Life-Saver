FAQ

Questions, answered

How is this different from a reminder app?

Reminders are passive — they're easy to ignore. RESCUE runs an agent loop that reasons about urgency, builds a real plan, drafts and decomposes work, and escalates as deadlines approach. It helps you act, not just remember.

What powers the agent?

Google's Gemini drives the reasoning, planning, and drafting via structured output. A built-in local engine is the fallback so it keeps working even without an API key.

Does my data stay private?

Your tasks and settings are stored locally in your browser and persist across refreshes. Your API key never leaves your device.

Is it free?

Yes — launch it in your browser, no credit card. Bring your own Gemini key for full agentic reasoning, or use the local engine.
